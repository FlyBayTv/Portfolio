Fake News Web Site

The "Fake News" website was not only created by me but also by a friend of mine. This website emphasizes the importance of identifying and combating fake news. Fake news refers to false or misleading information presented as factual news, often spread through various media channels.

The theme of the website was taken from Bootstrap, ensuring that our website is supported on various devices and platforms. Bootstrap provides a responsive design framework that allows the site to adapt and display properly on different screen sizes and resolutions.


The purpose of the "Fake News" website is to raise awareness about the prevalence of fake news and to provide resources on how to recognize and verify trustworthy information sources. We aim to educate visitors about the potential impact of misinformation and the importance of critical thinking and fact-checking. Through this website, we hope to empower individuals to become more discerning consumers of news and to contribute to a more informed society. By promoting media literacy and responsible news consumption, we believe we can play a part in combating the spread of fake news.
